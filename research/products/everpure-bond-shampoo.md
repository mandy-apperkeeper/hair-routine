## Executive Summary

The L'Oréal EverPure Bond Repair Shampoo is a sulfate-free cleanser built around a 6-surfactant gentle cleansing system led by Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, with citric acid as the "bond repair" active that creates new ionic crosslinks between keratin chains (peer-reviewed: +6.7-15% denaturation temperature increase). It also delivers amodimethicone via a standard emulsifier system for selective conditioning during the wash step, and contains a pseudoceramide (2-Oleamido-1,3-Octadecanediol) that mimics natural cuticle lipids. The primary-tier placement is validated: this shampoo cleanses without stripping, actively repairs via citric acid crosslinking, and conditions selectively — a fundamentally different mechanism than the sulfate-based Dove shampoos it replaced.
