INCIDecoder version also lists: Sodium Lauryl Sulfate (emulsifier at trace level), Sorbic Acid, Butylene Glycol, Iodopropynyl Butylcarbamate, Sodium Benzoate, Citronellol, Limonene, Hydroxycitronellal, Linalool

### Key structural observations:
- **PQ-69 at position 2** — extremely high concentration. This is the defining feature.
- Only 15 ingredients (very clean formula for a styling gel)
- NO silicones — safe for curl definition, no buildup concern from silicones
- NO drying alcohols — sulfate-free, paraben-free, silicone-free, phthalate-free
- "Rice Curl Complex" = rice extract + keratin amino acids + enzyme (marketing name for positions 7-11)
- SLS at trace level (INCIDecoder) is an emulsifier for the crosspolymer, not a cleanser
- Simple formula: hold polymer + film-former + humectant + protein + preservatives

## PQ-69 Mechanism (the key ingredient)

**From Glooshi (2026):**
- Made from vinyl caprolactam + vinylpyrrolidone + dimethylaminopropyl methacrylamide + methacryloylaminopropyl lauryldimonium chloride
- Positive charge bonds to negatively charged hair surface
- Forms "invisible, breathable film that locks a chosen style in place"
- "Helps hair resist frizz caused by humidity"
- Film is FLEXIBLE — "allows movement and touchability, preventing the stiff helmet effect"
- Enhances shine by smoothing hair surface

**From NaturallyCurly (Tonya McKay, 2023):**
- PQ-69 = AquaStyle 300 (by ISP/Ashland)
- "Used as the primary hold agent in a number of styling gels and foaming mousse products"
- Named products: Jessicurl Extreme Hold Spiralicious, Got2b Ultra Glued
- "Ingredient proven to resist the highest humidity"

**From AbsolutelyEverythingCurly (2026):**
- Polyquats "strongly bind to negatively charged hair, especially bleached or chemically treated hair"
- Can cause buildup — "some polyquats are notoriously difficult to remove"
- Best removal: C14-16 olefin sulfonate (which is in EverPure Clarifying Shampoo!)
- "Bind more tightly to low porosity hair long-term than to high porosity hair"

**From project's existing research (PHASE1, PHASE5, PRODUCT_RECOMMENDATIONS):**

## Important distinction: Flash Freeze vs Sculpting Gel

NYM makes TWO different gels in the Curl Talk line:
1. **Flash Freeze Gel** — PQ-69 based (humidity-resistant, hard cast) ← THIS IS MANDY'S
2. **Sculpting Gel** — VP/VA Copolymer based (flexible hold, NOT humidity-resistant)
3. **Fragrance & Protein Free Sculpting Gel** — VP/VA + carbomer (sensitive version)

The Flash Freeze is the ONLY one with PQ-69. The others are completely different products despite similar names.
